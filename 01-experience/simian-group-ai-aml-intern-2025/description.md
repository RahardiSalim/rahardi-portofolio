# AI/AML Intern at Simian Group

## Short Description

Built production-ready RAG system for Indonesian legal document retrieval in wealth management, achieving ~30% nDCG through hybrid search, reranking, and HyDE-based query expansion.

## Long Description

During my internship at Simian Group, I spearheaded the development of a sophisticated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system designed specifically for Indonesia's $350B wealth management sector. The challenge was clear: legal document retrieval in Indonesian required not just translation capabilities, but deep contextual understanding of regulatory nuances.

The breakthrough came through a multi-layered approach. I integrated hybrid search combining BM25's keyword precision with vector similarity's semantic understanding, then enhanced it with reranking algorithms and HyDE-based query expansion. This architecture achieved approximately 30% nDCG in ranking quality—a significant milestone for Indonesian legal retrieval systems.

But building the retrieval engine was only part of the story. I architected an end-to-end full-stack prototype that demonstrated how scalable AI deployments could work in practice, constructing pipelines from document processing through to an interactive UI complete with vector storage and visualization layers.

The research component was equally rigorous. I benchmarked over 10 large language models and rerankers, systematically evaluating efficiency across time, cost, and accuracy metrics. This led to implementing Graph RAG as an enhanced retrieval feature, pushing the boundaries of what was possible with deep learning prototypes.

To optimize the user experience, I developed a feedback loop prototype that incorporated user interaction tracking mechanisms, improving responsiveness by approximately 15% and laying groundwork for MLOps improvements.

Beyond the technical implementation, I conducted strategic analysis of AI adoption in Indonesia's wealth management landscape, identifying key opportunities while navigating complex regulatory considerations. This internship crystallized my understanding of how cutting-edge AI research translates into production systems that serve real business needs in emerging markets.
