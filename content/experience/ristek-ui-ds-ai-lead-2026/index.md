# Lead of Data Science & Artificial Intelligence SIG - RISTEK UI

## Short Description

Directing Data Science & AI Special Interest Group by managing technical training programs and guiding members through development of advanced machine learning projects.

## Long Description

Taking on the leadership of RISTEK UI's Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Special Interest Group means stewarding the next generation of AI practitioners at University of Indonesia. This isn't just about teaching algorithms or frameworks—it's about building a community where students transform from learners into builders.

As the SIG Lead, I direct both the strategic vision and tactical execution of our technical training programs. The challenge is designing a learning path that takes members from foundational concepts through to advanced machine learning projects they can showcase in portfolios and competitions.

The approach is hands-on and project-driven. Rather than endless lectures on theory, we emphasize building. Members work on real machine learning challenges, learning to navigate the messy reality of data cleaning, feature engineering, model selection, and deployment—the skills that separate classroom knowledge from production capabilities.

Guiding members through advanced projects means being part mentor, part architect, and part debugger. It's helping someone understand why their model isn't converging, suggesting alternative approaches when initial experiments fail, and celebrating when they finally achieve that breakthrough result.

But the role extends beyond technical mentorship. It's about creating space for experimentation, fostering collaboration between members with different strengths, and connecting our work with the broader ecosystem of data science education and industry in Indonesia.

The most rewarding moments come when members who joined with basic Python skills present sophisticated projects at competitions or land internships because of work they developed through the SIG. That progression—from curious beginner to confident practitioner—is what this leadership role is ultimately about.

RISTEK UI has a legacy of producing strong technical talent. As the DS & AI SIG Lead, I'm committed to continuing that tradition, ensuring our members don't just study machine learning—they master it.
